Need Help with the Form?

Welcome to WellnessCheck+! Here are a few tips to help you fill out the registration form:

Tip: All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required and must be filled out before submitting.

Field Guidelines:

  • Name: Only letters, dashes, and apostrophes are allowed.
  • Date of Birth: Must be a valid date. No future dates allowed.
  • SSN: Enter 9 digits. Dashes will be auto-formatted.
  • Email: Use a valid format like you@example.com.
  • Phone: Format as 123-456-7890.
  • Address: Include street number and name.
  • City: Enter your city name using only letters.
  • State: Select your state from the dropdown list.
  • Zip Code: Enter your 5-digit zip code.
  • Passwords: Must be at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and a number.
  • Remember Me: Check this to save your name for next time (48 hours).

Form Navigation:

After filling out all required fields, click the "Validate" button to check your form. If all information is correct, the "Submit" button will appear.

Cookie Information: This form uses cookies to remember your name for your convenience. Your information is stored locally on your device only and expires after 48 hours.

If you still have questions, reach out to our support team. We're here to help!
